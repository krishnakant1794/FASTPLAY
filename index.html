MIRZAPUR

Fantasy, Action

Seasons: 3 | Episodes: 11

A shocking incident at a wedding procession ignites a series of events entangling the lives of two families in the lawless city of Mirzapur.

MIRZAPUR: Detailed Synopsis

Mirzapur follows the escalating power struggle between the Tripathi and Pandit families, with a major turning point being a wedding procession incident that leads to a bloody confrontation. Kaleen, a millionaire carpet exporter and mafia don, seeks to maintain his dominance over Mirzapur, while his power-hungry son Munna vies for his father's legacy, often resorting to violence. The Pandit family, including the lawyer Ramakant and his sons Guddu and Bablu, become embroiled in this conflict, eventually leading to a brutal and unpredictable saga of power, greed, and revenge.
